Our Team

About the AWENS Team

The AWENS team is comprised of proven excellence in order to provide your organizations with the best in adult education, training, and certification. To meet and exceed the expectations of our clients, our staff comes from a diverse educational background ranging from global leadership, adult education to advanced office administration. This academic and technical expertise present in our organization allows our team to find personalized learning solutions that will help unlock the potential in your workforce.

AWENS has created a network of industr y partners allowing us to identify key trends in workplace education and training. Our team integrates those concepts into our platform, keeping our course materials current and our training competitive. AWENS is here to bring world class, customized education, and training to your workplace.

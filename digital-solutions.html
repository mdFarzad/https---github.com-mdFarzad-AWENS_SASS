AWENS provides the best in adult education; in-person and digitally.

Each educational journey is different and requires a unique approach. AWENS' digital solutions are specific to an organization's needs. AWENS' diverse experience in the world of digital education allows us to create custom, interactive education solutions for any and all subject matter.

Test Knowledge
Assesments - Using multiple-choice to matching, sequencing, drag-and-drop and more
Engage With Video
Video lectures, watch-and-repeat tutorials, software simulations and other visual content that will motivate your learners
Share & Collect Data
Quiz results to be automatically sent to an email or server, or configure JavaScript to forward the results to your learning management system.
Access Anywhere/Anytime
Content displays perfectly on any device, including PCs, Macs, tablets, and smartphones (with Windows, iOS, and Android OS).
Offline & Online
Learners can download courses to their mobile devices and access them offline.

Our digital experts assesses your organization's learning needs and create a customized learning platform based on your content.
